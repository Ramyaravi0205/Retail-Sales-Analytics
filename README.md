# Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard – Power BI

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze retail business performance across sales, customers, products, stores, and customer segments. The dashboard provides actionable insights through dynamic visualizations, KPI tracking, and trend analysis to support data-driven decision-making. 

## 🎯 Objectives

* Monitor overall sales performance and revenue trends.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior and segmentation.
* Identify top-performing products, brands, and categories.
* Evaluate store-wise and state-wise sales performance.
* Support business growth through data-driven insights.

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop**
* **Power Query**
* **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)**
* **Data Modeling**
* **Interactive Visualizations**

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1. Overview

Provides a high-level summary of key business metrics including Total Sales, Revenue, Orders, Average Order Value, Customer Segments, and Product Performance. 

### 2. Sales Analysis

* Total Sales Trend by Year and Month
* Sales by Product and Brand
* State-wise Sales Performance
* Average Order Value Analysis
* Product and Store Performance Tracking

### 3. Customer Analysis

* Revenue by Product Category
* Active Customer Analysis
* Order Distribution
* Store-wise Quantity Sold
* Shipping Status Insights

### 4. Segment Analysis

* Customer Segmentation (Loyal, New, At-Risk)
* Segment-wise Revenue Analysis
* Category-wise Customer Distribution
* City-wise Segment Analysis

## 📈 Key Insights

* Tracked **₹8.58M Total Revenue** across multiple states and stores.
* Identified top-selling products, brands, and categories.
* Analyzed customer segments to understand retention and risk patterns.
* Evaluated regional sales performance for business optimization.
* Enabled interactive filtering and drill-down analysis for deeper insights.
